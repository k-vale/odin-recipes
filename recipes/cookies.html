World's Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Easy to Make, Even Easier to Eat!

Ingredients you will need:

  1. flour
  2. chocolate chips
  3. butter
  4. eggs
  5. vanilla
  6. baking soda
  7. water
  8. salt
  9. sugar

Just follow these steps:

  1. Mix wet ingredients together (butter, eggs, vanilla and water).
  2. In a separate bowl, mis dry ingredients together (flour, baking soda, chocolate chips, salt and sugar).
