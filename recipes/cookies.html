World's Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

Easy to Make, Even Easier to Eat!

Ingredients you will need:

  • flour
  • baking soda
  • chocolate chips
  • salt
  • sugar
  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • water

Just follow these steps:

  1. Heat your oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix wet ingredients together (butter, eggs, vanilla and water).
  3. In a separate bowl, mix dry ingredients together (flour, baking soda, chocolate chips, salt and sugar).
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, mix until fully combined.
  5. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for about 12 minutes or until golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your freshly baked cookies!
