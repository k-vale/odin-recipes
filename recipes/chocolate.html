Chocolate Covered Strawberries

Delicious chocolate covered strawberries anytime

Ingredients to gather:

  • 1 pound strawberries
  • chocolate

Just follow these steps:

  1. Wash strawberries and towel dry.
  2. Heat chocolate in a bowl above boiling water.
  3. Once chocolate is fully melted, dip strawberries in the warm chocolate.
  4. Let cool on a wire rack.
  5. Once chocolate solidifies, enjoy your treat!
