Decadent Carrot Cake

A carrot cake that really takes the cake!

Ingredients needed:

  • sugar
  • oil
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • flour
  • baking soda
  • cinnamon
  • salt
  • shredded carrots
  • pecans
  • butter and cream cheese

Three Simple steps:

  1. Make the batter: Beat the wet ingredients and then mix with the dry ingredients, then add the carrots and pecans.
  2. Bake the cake: Pour batter into greased pans and bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees F.
  3. Frost the cake: Smooth frosting all over cake and sprinkle pecans on top.
